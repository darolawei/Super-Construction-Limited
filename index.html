Super Construction

Super Construction Services Ltd is a Papua New Guinea–owned construction company delivering quality building, civil, and infrastructure projects. Managing Director, Mr. Willie Dus, from Yaken Lower Mendi, Southern Highlands Province, holds a Bachelor’s Degree in Environmental Health from the University of Papua New Guinea. With over 10 years of experience, he worked with OTML and later served as General Manager at Cane Construction Company before establishing his own company. The company has successfully delivered residential buildings, road and bridge projects in Nipa, Port Moresby, Lae, and recently in Kiribati..
